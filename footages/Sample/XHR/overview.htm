Tips and tricks

Did you know that Google Reader has lots of useful keyboard shortcuts? Here are some of them:

  • j/k: next/previous item
  • n/p: scan down/up (list only)
  • o/enter: expand/collapse (list only)
  • s: star item
  • <Shift> + s: share item
  • v: view original
  • m: mark item as read/unread
  • r: refresh
  • f: toggle full screen mode
  • <Shift> + a: mark all as read
